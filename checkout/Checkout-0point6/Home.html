* After the user attempts to add an item (called "Random Item) to the cart, the ZIP code dialogue appears. This checks whether we can ship the item and either says "Yes, that can be shipped to your ZIP: how many would you like?" or "No, sorry, that can't go to your ZIP."

 

* In the latter case the item the user is looking at will be replaced with a suggested substitute item. We may mention this in the "sorry we can't ship the thing you wanted to you" dialogue.

 

* All items not shippable to the ZIP entered at the ZIP dialogue are filtered out of the user's browsing experience after we get the ZIP.
